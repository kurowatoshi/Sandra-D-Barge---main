General Mission Statements:

· To glorify God by building a loving and welcoming community of faith, inspiring spiritual growth, and serving our neighbors.
· To share the message of Jesus Christ through worship, education, and outreach, fostering hope and transformation in the lives of all.

Mission Statements with a focus on Outreach:

· To be a beacon of hope and compassion in our community, providing spiritual nourishment, social services, and a helping hand to those in need.
· To inspire and equip our congregation to be active participants in social justice initiatives, working towards a more just and loving world.

Mission Statements with a focus on Youth Ministry:

· To create a vibrant and engaging space for young people to explore their faith, build strong connections, and develop as Christian leaders.
· To nurture the spiritual growth of children and youth, fostering a lifelong love of learning and service to God.

Mission Statements with a focus on Inclusivity:

· To celebrate the diversity of God's creation by welcoming and embracing people of all backgrounds, beliefs, and walks of life.
· To create a safe and inclusive space where everyone feels loved, valued, and empowered to grow in their faith.
